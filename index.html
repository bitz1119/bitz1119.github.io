01 The Operator's Note

A builder who sells and ships.

Engineer by training, operator by trade. I write production code on Mondays and pitch enterprise clients on Tuesdays — and I think the best founders should still be able to do both.

“I like the messy middle — the half between a Google Doc and a paying enterprise client. That's where the real product gets built, and that's where I do my best work.”
— A working philosophy

Bittoo Aggarwal is the Co-Founder and CTO of Wavexcel, where he architects enterprise AI products for clients like Siemens and Propgrow — covering real-time video intelligence, process automation, and time-series forecasting on AWS.

Before Wavexcel he was a founding member and Vice President at Geekster, where he helped scale revenue from ₹30L to ₹10Cr ARR, raise $1.3M from Inflection Point Ventures, FirstPort and Mynavi, and led a team of 45+ across Learning & Placements.

Earlier, an SDE at Amazon's Transportation org — owning tier-1 services that powered 10+ APIs across 250+ internal teams — and a software engineer at ION, working on reference data products used by 60+ global financial institutions.

On the side: workshops at IISc Bangalore, IIT Delhi, Bombay and Madras, content as @codewithbittoo, and a string of national hackathon wins.
